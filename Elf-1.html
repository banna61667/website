Mammoth Trip

I had an amazing time during this trip at Mammoth.It was surprisingly going to be my first time going to Mammoth, so I was unsure as to how cold it would be and the landscape of the trip. It was also going to be my first time mountain biking, and my initial expectation of mountain biking was a bunch of bikers quickly biking down a steep hill while trying to weave through trees and rocks. This trip beat my expectations and proved to me how fun mountain biking truly is, especially with different people

Reflection

My class trip was very fun as I was able to meet new people when we were mountain biking, hiking, and at the campgrounds. I had many firsts during this trip, and mountain biking was the biggest of all of them. Although I got hurt, slipped, and fell along the pathway towards our destination, my new friends helped me and gave me time to learn.


A Map of Mammoth

Mammoth has been through a lot on a geographical and historical level. I remember reading about wildfires and natural disaasters that occured in Mammoth. It was very interesting, yet devasting to learn about.

There were many other geographical devastations that occured in Mammoth, such as a grand fault line and the change in lake sizes. In the end, it is breathtaking to look at the landscape and natural life that surrounded us during the trip. I'll never forget the views that I saw at Mammoth.

This program was very good when it comes to biking actually fun trails, feading us throughout the day, and giving all of us time to relate and do whatever. I really appreciate the organization and clear schedule that the adult leaders were following throughout the trip. I was finally able to do actual biking during this trip in Mammoth.
